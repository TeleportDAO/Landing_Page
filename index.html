Teleport Your Assets

Transfer your tokens, NFTs or data from the source chain to the target chain, or exchange them for other assets on the target chain.

Simple as transferring tokens on a single chain.

Fully controlled by smart contracts.

Automated market maker pricing model.

Tune Your Settlement Speed

TeleportDAO has three types of settlements:

Normal: settle your assets on the target chain after your request gets finalized on the source chain.

Fast: settle your assets on the target chain before your request gets finalized on the source chain.

Instant: settle your assets on the target chain instantly by staking TeleportDAO token (TDT).
